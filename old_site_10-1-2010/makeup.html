Make Up for Segment 1

from Jan 9th-Feb 3rd, 2007
in Kirkland, WA

Although the 1st segment of the Seattle Eastside Feldenkrais® Training Program(SEFT II) began in Fall 2006, its not too late to enroll in this 4 year Training by taking our SEGMENT I MAKE UP and joining in on SEGMENT 2, March 5-30, 2007.

This SEGMENT I MAKE UP runs for 4 weeks, 2 nights a week, with 1 intensive weekend. The MAKE UP will prepare new trainees to enter into SEFT II SEGMENT 2 program easily. Both the make up and the training
itself will take place in Kirkland.

The SEFT II Program itself meets 1 month, 2x a year, 9-3pm, M-F, weekends off.

2007 MAKE UP DATES (Applicants must attend all dates to obtain credit for Segment 1)

This SEGMENT I MAKE UP will be based on the first 100 hours of the SEFT II training. Some of the material will be directly taken from the DVDs and portions taught live by SEFT II co-directors, Angel Di Benedetto & Richard Corbeil. Additionally, the new trainees will have access and are required to study the audio-visual recording of the entire segment at home. Cost for this make up will equivalent to a Training Segment’s fee of $1975.

Space in MAKE UP is limited. For an appointment, interview, or more information about how to apply into our SEFT II Program, please contact:

Angel Di Benedetto, GCFT
Educational Director of SEFT I & II

Log onto our website www.feldenkraisinseattle .com, click on Professional Training and download our Training Brochure to learn more about SEFT II.

It is not a prerequisite to enroll in this Segment 1 Make Up in order to enter into the 2nd Segment of the Seattle Eastside Feldenkrais® Training Program. However a student will need to make up Segment 1 in some other Guild Certified Feldenkrais® Training (approved by the Educational Director) in order to graduate and become a Guild Certified Feldenkrais® Practitioner.
