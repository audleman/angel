HOW TO START ATM CLASSES
HOW TO DEVELOP A PRACTICE FROM YOUR CLASSES

Knowing how to teach ATM classes are one thing, but knowing how to start a class and bring people in is an entirely different ball of wax. Through the years and from past experiences in sales and marketing, I've devised some very easy strategies on how to promote and ways to gather folks to one's Feldenkrais® classes.

Many times Trainees and/or Practitioners relocate and start from nothing, or take a break and loose momentum. How does one start again? My theory is to start with a large class and from that base, build your FI practice. My cassette shows you how.
