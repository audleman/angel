Angel trained personally with Dr. Feldenkrais in Amherst and has been in full practice since 1983. She gathered extensive teaching experience, working early in her career as an Assistant Trainer in 29 programs, and later as a Trainer in over 23 different trainings worldwide. Her teaching focuses on developing creativity to enhance artistic expression, self confidence, personal awareness and one's quality of life. While maintaining a private practice in the Seattle area, she devotes much of her time forming new Feldenkrais Practitioners in the U.S.A., Canada, Europe, Israel, New Zealand and Australia.
