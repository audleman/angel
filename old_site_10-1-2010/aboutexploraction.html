Based in the greater Seattle area, ExplorACTION was formed in 2001 by Angel DiBenedetto and Richard Corbeil to provide a wide range of services based on the Feldenkrais Method® of somatic education.
These services include:

  • Individual sessions of Functional Integration®
  • Awareness Through Movement® classes
  • Professional training in the Feldenkrais Method
  • Advanced training for postgraduates
  • Public workshops
  • Practitioners supervision and mentoring
  • Onsite consulting

You can get information on these services by clicking on Feldenkrais Services on the menu or by contacting us.
